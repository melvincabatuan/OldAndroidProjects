DISMATHOID 
Knight or Knave?

DISMATHOID is a quiz app dedicated to discrete mathematics students of all ages. Knights always tell the truth while knaves always lie. Thus, you get a point by pressing the knight button if the statement is true or pressing the knave button if the statement is false.
